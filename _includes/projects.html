Projects 2012

Various ongoing projects I have been creating on my free time. Please contact me If you're interested in getting involved.

By combining the universal application of well-established learning platforms with the dynamic and portable nature of virtual physics simulations, Physics Academy seeks to build next-generation educational technologies to benefit the worldwide student community.

Learning and student interest is best attained through engagement and interaction. Unfortunately today's educational model lacks both. Physics Academy was to created to change this by providing the most effective and enjoyable way to learn and to teach physics at any level.
