I have broad interests but my core talents are in the design and synthesis of analog and digital circuits both from a perspective of fabrication, layout, and high level design for next-generation biological technology. Feel free to check out my resume, as well as my patents and publications.

I also love to code and have written a panoply of software a variety of technologies over the years. This has included everything from image processing, to robotic control systems, and even a live javascript circuit simulator. My development specialties lie in the development of "full stack" web frameworks. This includes everything from user-experience to database and all that lies between. Check out the links to the left to get an idea of my skills with various technologies, or better yet, check out my portfolio.

My greatest accomplishments have arisen from when I have been intellectually challenged and mentally engaged. I keep a busy schedule, but if you feel my skillsets are in line with a project you are working on, let's talk.
